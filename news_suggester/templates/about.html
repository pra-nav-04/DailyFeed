Needs of Personalized News

  1. In the last decade, there has been a rapid growth in the field of Mass Media. This has resulted in a massive increase in the amount of news that is being generated and posted by the various news sources .

  2. Various platforms like Google News, Yahoo News, Bing News etc. have been created so, that a user can get information relevant to his/her topics of interest and in a time saving manner.

  3. The issues with these platforms do not lie in the content they provide but the method they use to generate a news feed for a particular User. For example, Google uses a User’s search history to generate the content. So, many a times the content turns out to be irrelevant or out of User’s personal interest.

  4. Many other such platforms use google search history to generate their feed which might have not been approved by the user. Thus, this becomes interference in someone’s privacy.

Personalized News Feed Generator

  1. We Have developed a Web Application that provides personalized news & event updates to its users without losing out on time or having to go through irrelevant content.

  2. Our Website uses Machine Learning Algorithm like Support Vector Machine for classifying the category of the News.

  3. News articles will be recommended based on clustering of similar articles, predicting their category, content similarity & keyword extraction.

  4. The information regarding the user’s interests and likes will be taken initially when the User does login on the website for the first time. Based on this information the news feed will be generated for the user.

  5. All information regarding the past clicks, likes, engagement and the amount of time that a user spends on a particular article on this website will be taken into consideration and will be prioritized for future content and predictions.
